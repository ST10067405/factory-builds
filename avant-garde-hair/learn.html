Bold colour, done right

Fashion colour and big transformations are Amber's happy place. Here's how to get there — and keep it looking incredible between visits.

Common questions

How long does a colour correction take?

Usually two to three sessions, spaced out to protect your hair. We give you a realistic plan and quote at the consultation rather than promising a one-visit miracle.

Can you fix a box-dye or home-colour disaster?

Very often, yes — bring photos and be honest about what went on the hair. Corrections take patience, but we would rather do it safely than fry your hair.

How do I stop fashion colours fading so fast?

Wash less and cooler, use a sulphate-free colour-safe shampoo, top up with a colour-depositing conditioner, and book the odd gloss refresh.
