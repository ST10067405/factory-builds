Hair care for Hillcrest & the Upper Highway

Practical, coastal-climate hair care from the Salon Highwaves team — for salt air, humidity, and everything Old Main Rd throws at your style.

Common questions

How often should I get a trim in Hillcrest's humid climate?

Most people do well with a trim every 6–8 weeks, though coastal humidity can make ends feel rougher sooner. Regular trims keep style and shape looking sharp between visits.

Will salt air and humidity affect my colour?

Yes — coastal air and UV exposure can fade colour and dry out lengths faster than inland climates. A sulphate-free shampoo, UV-protecting spray, and regular gloss or toner top-ups help colour last longer near the coast.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Photos of styles or colours you like (and ones you don't), a sense of your hair's history (colour, chemical treatments), and a realistic idea of the upkeep you're willing to do at home.
