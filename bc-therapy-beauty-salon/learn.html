Skin & Beauty Notes

Practical, honest reads on skincare and beauty treatments for Moseley Park and Pinetown, from the team at BC Therapy - Beauty Salon.

Common questions

How often should I get a facial?

As a general guide, every 4–6 weeks keeps most skin types in good condition, though this varies by skin goal and treatment type.

What should I avoid before a waxing appointment?

Avoid sun exposure, retinol products and exfoliating for a few days beforehand, and make sure hair has enough length to be gripped properly.

How do I make my nails last longer between visits?

Wear gloves for household chores, moisturise your cuticles daily, and avoid using your nails as tools.
