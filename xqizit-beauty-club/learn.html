Common questions

Where is Xqizit Beauty Club?

We're at 7 Harry Gwala Road, Westville, Durban. Directions are on the Visit section of the main site.

How does the membership work?

Xqizit is a members' beauty club — one monthly membership covers your weekly gel hands and toes plus brow, lip, sides and underarm waxing, with optional wash and set add-ons. The club confirms the current membership details when you join.

What is included in the membership?

Weekly gel manicure and pedicure, and brow, lip, sides and underarm waxing. Wash and set is an optional add-on. The final menu is set by the club.

How do I join?

Call or WhatsApp 073 565 1752. Online joining is coming to this site soon.
