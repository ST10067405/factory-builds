Common questions

How often should a fade be touched up?

Most fades hold their shape for two to three weeks before the line loses definition. Fine, fast-growing hair may need a touch-up closer to two weeks; coarser hair can often stretch to four.

What should I do before a hot-towel shave?

Come with a clean, dry face — no need to pre-shave. Avoid heavy moisturiser beforehand so the hot towel and lather can do their job properly.
