Common questions

Do I need to close the mains stopcock during a burst pipe?

Yes — closing the mains stopcock immediately is the single biggest thing that limits water damage during a burst pipe. Do that first, before anything else.

Why do older Durban homes have plumbing problems more often?

Many older Durban homes still run on galvanised steel pipework from decades ago, which corrodes from the inside and eventually fails without much external warning — unlike modern copper or PVC.

Should I worry about my drains before summer rains?

Yes — Durban's heavy summer storms expose slow-draining or partially blocked drains fast. A check before the rainy season is far cheaper than a flooded yard during it.
