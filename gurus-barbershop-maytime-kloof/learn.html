The Guru's Journal

Grooming guides for Kloof village

Six quick reads on fades, beards and getting the most out of your visit to the chair — written for the men of Kloof and the Upper Highway.

Common questions

How often should I get a haircut?

For most short styles and fades, every 3-4 weeks keeps the shape sharp. Longer or low-maintenance styles can stretch to 6-8 weeks. Your barber can suggest a rhythm that suits your hair type and how fast it grows.

How do I keep a fade looking sharp between visits?

Wash regularly, keep the hairline tidy with a trimmer if you're confident, and avoid letting the sides grow out too far before your next visit — a fade loses its crispness fastest around the neckline and temples.

What should I expect from a hot-towel shave?

A hot towel softens the beard and opens the pores before the blade, making the shave closer and more comfortable. Expect a slower, more relaxed process than a quick self-shave at home, often finished with a cooling balm.
