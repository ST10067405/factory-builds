Meet Dr Balaram Bhikha.

Konfident at Kloof is led by Dr Balaram Bhikha, a Wits-qualified dentist who believes good dental care should be calm, honest and within reach. The practice was opened to give Kloof families a friendly local option — modern, welcoming and never rushed.

From your first visit, the focus is simple: explain things clearly, keep you comfortable, and look after your smile for the long term.
