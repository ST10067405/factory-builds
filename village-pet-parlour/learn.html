The Parlour Journal · Dog Care

Happy dogs, well cared for

Grooming tips the team at Village Pet Parlour shares with pet parents — simple, practical advice to keep your dog comfortable between visits.

Common questions

How often should I get my dog groomed?

Most dogs do well with a full groom every 4–8 weeks, though it depends on coat type and lifestyle. Short, smooth coats can often stretch to 8–12 weeks, while curly, double or long coats that mat easily are usually best every 4–6 weeks. Ask us at your next visit and we'll suggest a schedule for your dog's coat.

My dog is nervous — can you still groom them?

Yes, absolutely — it's something we're known for. We go at your dog's pace, use plenty of breaks and reassurance, and never rush a nervous pup. Many of the dogs we see regularly started out anxious and are now completely relaxed on the table.

What should I bring to my dog's grooming appointment?

Just your dog, their lead, and any vaccination or health details we don't already have on file. If your dog has a favourite treat or toy that helps them settle, feel free to bring that along too.
