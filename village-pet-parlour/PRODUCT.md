# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local dog owners in Kloof and the Upper Highway (KwaZulu-Natal), almost always on a phone.
They're looking for a groomer they can trust with a pet that may be nervous or new to grooming.
The job to be done: quickly decide "these people will be gentle and careful with my dog," then
call, WhatsApp, or navigate to book. Many are first-time or anxious-pet owners who need
reassurance more than a feature list.

## Product Purpose

**The site** turns local pet owners into bookings/visits by conveying calm, gentle, genuinely
caring grooming — with real proof (4.6★, 43 reviews) rather than claims.

**The demo (meta purpose):** this is a speculative Tier-0 concept built by Arkatype Digital to
**win Village Pet Parlour as a paying client**. It must feel instantly credible and clearly a cut
above a template, while staying recognisably *theirs* so the owner says "yes, that's us." Success =
the owner signs. This dual purpose (convert pet-owners / impress the owner) governs every call.

## Brand Personality

Warm, calm, trustworthy. Voice is friendly and reassuring, plain-spoken, a little playful but never
silly — the tone of a person who is visibly good with anxious animals. The feeling a worried pet
parent should get on arrival: *"my dog will be safe, relaxed, and happy here."* Emotion over
transaction; care over polish.

## Anti-references

- **Cutesy clipart clichés** — cartoon paws everywhere, comic-sans energy, rainbow pastels. Reads
  unserious for real money. (The current floating-paw motif must stay restrained and tasteful, or go.)
- **Sterile vet clinic** — clinical white-and-teal, cold, medical. This is grooming and care, not surgery.
- **Generic groomer template** — indistinguishable from every other pet site; no point of view.
- Keep warmth *human*, not cartoonish; keep care *evident*, not clinical.

## Design Principles

- **Elevate, don't reinvent.** Sharpen craft — type scale, spacing rhythm, hierarchy, motion — inside
  the committed identity (navy `#1F2A44` + mustard `#E0A83D` + warm cream, Baloo 2 / Nunito Sans).
  The owner must still see themselves in it. Identity-preservation wins over greenfield ambition.
- **Trust is the product.** Every decision should make a nervous pet-parent feel their dog is safe.
  Warmth beats slickness; if a "premium" move makes it feel colder, it loses.
- **Real, never invented.** Only genuine data — real Google-review quotes, `R —` until the parlour
  supplies prices, draft markers on anything unconfirmed (hours). Factory SOP; non-negotiable.
- **Ship-ready or it doesn't ship.** Any change must still pass `scripts/preflight.js`:
  compartmentalised unique assets (no image or journal shared with another client), Comms Bridge +
  journal present, `noindex` + draft ribbon + `prefers-reduced-motion`. Design freedom lives inside the gate.
- **Show the care, don't just claim it.** Lean on the real proof (calms nervous dogs, "happy vibe,"
  adorable results). Imagery is the known weak point — only disclosed stock exists today — so the
  design must make genuine parlour photos drop in cleanly the moment they're supplied.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 — watch mustard `#E0A83D` on cream and muted-navy body copy, both of which
sit close to the line. Visible focus states (present). `prefers-reduced-motion` fully honoured (paw
trail and reveal animations already disable). Mobile-first: most local pet owners arrive on a phone,
so the phone layout is the primary design target, not an afterthought.
