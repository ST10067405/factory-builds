The Story Behind Our Beans

Coffee

Specialty coffee is about more than caffeine; it's about consistency, care, and respect for where the bean came from. At Moto, every cup starts long before it reaches your table.

Sourcing. We work with roasters who can tell us exactly where a bean comes from, how it was processed, and why it tastes the way it does. That traceability matters; it's the difference between "coffee" and a cup with a story.

Dialing in. Our baristas calibrate the grind and extraction throughout the day as humidity and bean freshness shift. It's a small, constant adjustment most guests never notice, but it's why your flat white tastes the same brilliant way every time.

Why it matters. A great cup of coffee should taste intentional. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, every single morning.

Pop in and ask your barista what's on pour, we love talking coffee.

Building the Perfect Brunch Plate

Food

All-day breakfast sounds simple until you've tried to keep it consistently great from 7am to close. Here's how we think about it.

Balance first. Every plate we serve balances something rich (eggs, avocado, halloumi) with something bright (lemon, herbs, tomato) and something with texture (toasted sourdough, crisp bacon). That contrast is what makes a plate satisfying rather than heavy.

Freshness, always. We prep in small batches throughout the day rather than holding large quantities; it's more work, but it means your 2pm breakfast tastes as fresh as the 7am order.

Built for sharing. Our brunch menu is designed for tables that order three different plates and pass them around. That's the Moto way: communal, generous, unhurried.

Come hungry, we'll take care of the rest.

Hosting Your Next Event at Moto

Events

Birthdays, baby showers, work catch-ups, baby moon brunches: our space has hosted all of it. Here's what to know if you're thinking of booking Moto for your next gathering.

Group bookings. We can accommodate groups for breakfast or brunch sittings, just give us a heads up via WhatsApp so we can prep the right amount of space and staff.

Menus for groups. For larger parties we can put together a set menu or pre-order system so everyone's order arrives together, no one waiting while the rest of the table eats.

Make it yours. Bring your own decor, cake, or balloons; we're happy to help make the space feel right for your occasion.

WhatsApp us with your date and headcount and we'll sort the rest.
