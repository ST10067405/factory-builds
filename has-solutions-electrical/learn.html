Common questions

What areas does H.A.S. Solutions cover?

We serve Forest Hills, Hillcrest and the surrounding Upper Highway in Durban. If you're nearby and not sure, send a WhatsApp to 072 408 2155 and ask.

Do you issue a Certificate of Compliance (COC)?

Yes. We carry out electrical work to standard and can issue a Certificate of Compliance where one is required, such as when a property is being sold.

My DB board keeps tripping — is that dangerous?

A trip is a safety device doing its job, but repeated tripping means something needs attention. Switch off, avoid overloading, and call an electrician to find the real cause rather than just resetting it.

How do I book H.A.S. Solutions?

Call or WhatsApp 072 408 2155. Tell us what's happening and we'll guide you from there.
