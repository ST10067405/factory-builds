Common questions

How far ahead should I book a facial?

A few days ahead is usually enough, but book earlier around weekends and holidays when slots fill fastest. Same-week bookings are often possible for smaller treatments.

What should I do before a lash or brow appointment?

Arrive with a clean face, free of mascara or brow makeup, and skip caffeine beforehand if you know your eyes tend to flutter — it makes fine work easier and faster.

How do I keep gel nails looking fresh for longer?

Wear gloves for cleaning and gardening, apply cuticle oil every few days, and avoid using your nails as tools — most chips start at the edge from everyday knocks.
