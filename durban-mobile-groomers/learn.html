Common questions

How often should I book a mobile groom?

It depends on breed and coat, but most dogs do well with a groom every 4 to 8 weeks. Shorter-coated dogs can often stretch longer; heavy-shedding or curly coats usually need it more often to avoid matting.

Do I need to prepare anything before the groomer arrives?

A bit of space near a tap or outdoor point helps, plus somewhere for your dog to relax afterwards. Beyond that, mobile grooming is built to fit around your home as it is.

Is mobile grooming less stressful for my dog?

Many dogs find it calmer — no car ride, no waiting room, and no other dogs to react to. Nervous dogs in particular often settle faster in a familiar space.
