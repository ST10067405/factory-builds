Hair Notes for Sarnia

Practical, everyday hair-care guidance from The Hair Design Studio — written for Farningham Ridge, Sarnia and the wider Pinetown climate.

Common questions

How often should I get a haircut to keep it healthy?

As a general guide, most hair benefits from a trim every 6–8 weeks to remove split ends before they travel up the shaft — finer or damaged hair may need it sooner, while longer, healthier hair can often stretch to 10–12 weeks.

What's the difference between balayage and highlights?

Highlights are foiled sections lightened uniformly from root to tip, giving a more consistent, all-over effect. Balayage is hand-painted freehand, so colour is placed to look sun-kissed and grows out softly with less obvious regrowth lines.

How can I protect my colour between salon visits?

Use a sulfate-free, colour-safe shampoo, wash in cooler water, and add a UV-protecting leave-in when you'll be in the sun. Reducing heat-styling frequency and using a heat protectant also slows fading noticeably.
