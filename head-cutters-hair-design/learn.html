Common questions

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your style and hair type, but most people do well with a trim every 8 to 12 weeks. Shorter, precise shapes can need attention closer to the 6-week mark to hold their line.

How do I stop my colour fading in Durban's heat and humidity?

Wash with a sulfate-free, colour-safe shampoo, keep washes lukewarm rather than hot, and use a leave-in UV protectant on days you're in the sun. Gentle handling between visits makes a bigger difference than any single product.

What should I bring to a first consultation?

A couple of reference photos of the look you want (and one you don't), an honest account of any past colour or chemical treatments, and a sense of how much upkeep you're realistically willing to do.
