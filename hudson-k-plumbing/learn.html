Common questions

What areas do you cover?

We serve Kloof and the surrounding Upper Highway area in Durban. We're mobile, so we come to you.

What should I do in a plumbing emergency?

Close your main water stopcock to stop the flow, switch the geyser off at the DB board if hot water is involved, then call us on 031 764 3734.

Do you repair and install geysers?

Yes. We repair geysers that leak, trip or give no hot water, and we also handle replacements and new installations.

How do I get hold of you?

Call 031 764 3734, or message us on WhatsApp. Phone is the fastest way to reach a plumber.
