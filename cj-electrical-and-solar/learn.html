Straight answers for Durban homes

Load-shedding, DB boards, solar retrofits and the questions Durban homeowners keep asking — written plainly, no jargon.

Common questions

How many load-shedding stages does Durban see, and does it matter for my wiring?

Load-shedding stages control how often and how long the power cuts — the electrical impact comes from the repeated on/off switching itself, which is worth understanding regardless of the stage.

Do I need an electrician to add solar to my home, or can I DIY it?

Connecting solar to your home's wiring and distribution board involves live electrical work, so it should be handled by a qualified electrician rather than as a DIY project.

Why does my DB board keep tripping when the power comes back on?

Repeated switching during load-shedding can strain circuits and DB boards over time, which is a common reason boards trip more often as outages continue — an electrician can identify the specific cause.
