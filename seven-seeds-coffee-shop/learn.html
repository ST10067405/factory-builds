Common questions

What's the difference between a flat white and a cappuccino?

Both start with espresso and steamed milk, but a flat white uses less milk with fine, glossy microfoam for a stronger coffee taste, while a cappuccino has more foam and a thicker, airier texture.

What is pour-over coffee?

Pour-over is a manual filter brewing method where hot water is poured slowly and evenly over ground coffee in a paper or cloth filter, giving a cleaner, lighter cup than espresso.

How should I store coffee beans at home?

Keep beans in an airtight container away from light, heat and moisture, and buy only what you'll use in a couple of weeks so they stay fresh.
