Our Ethos

You are a guest,
not a number

Carl Putz — known in Kloof simply as The Bowtie Man — has been doing hair for over four decades. In a world of back-to-back, rush-through appointments, Carl made a different choice: every client gets his full attention, for as long as it takes.

Your visit begins the moment you walk in. A drink is offered, conversation is easy, and your opinion is asked — not assumed. The head massage isn't an extra; it's just what happens here.
