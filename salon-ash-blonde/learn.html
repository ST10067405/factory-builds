Common questions

How long does ash blonde colour usually last?

Ash and cool-toned blondes typically hold their tone for 4–6 weeks before warmth starts to creep back in, depending on hair porosity, wash frequency and water hardness. A toning gloss between full colour visits helps stretch that window.

Why does blonde hair turn brassy?

Blonde hair carries warm underlying pigment (yellow/orange) that shows through as the cool ash toner fades. UV exposure, chlorine, hard water and sulphate shampoos all speed up that fade, which is why aftercare matters as much as the initial colour service.

What's the difference between balayage and foils?

Balayage is hand-painted freehand for a soft, sun-kissed graduation with minimal regrowth line. Foils isolate sections for more precise, consistent lightening — better for all-over brightness or a crisper contrast. Many colourists combine both in one service.
