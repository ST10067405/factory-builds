Brew better coffee at home

The three things that change everything: a 1:16 coffee-to-water ratio, fresh beans ground right before you brew, and water just off the boil (~93°C). Tastes weak? Grind finer. Bitter? Go coarser. That’s 90% of it.

Why this page earns its keep

Each guide is written as a clear, factual answer to something people Google, so the café shows up for searches the homepage never could (“how to make better coffee at home”, “best café to work from in Kloof”), and AI assistants like ChatGPT can quote it. Every post carries FAQ + Article schema, links back to the menu and booking, and doubles as ready-made social content. Helpful first, supplier shout-outs where it’s honest, never thin SEO filler.
