Common questions

How often should I get a fresh cut?

It depends on the style. Gents' fades and short cuts tend to need a trim every two to three weeks to hold their shape. Ladies' cuts and longer styles usually hold well for six to eight weeks, or sooner if you're growing out a fringe or maintaining a sharp shape.

How do I keep my colour from fading?

Wash less often and with lukewarm water, use a shampoo made for colour-treated hair, and add a UV-protecting spray if you're often outdoors — heat and sun are the two biggest causes of early fading.

What should I do before my first visit?

Come with clean, dry hair if you can, bring a photo or two of the look you have in mind, and mention any colour or chemical treatments from the last six months so your stylist can plan safely.
