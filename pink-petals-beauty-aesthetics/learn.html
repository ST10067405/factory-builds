Little bits of studio know-how from Pink Petals — how to make a lash set go the distance, pick the right brow treatment, and keep your skin happy in Kloof's weather.

Common questions

Where is Pink Petals?

We're at Shop 231/232, Village Mall, Kloof. Directions are on the Visit section of the main site.

How long do lash extensions take?

A full new set is a relaxed appointment — set aside a couple of hours. Infills are quicker. The studio confirms exact timing when you book.

Which brow treatment is right for me?

It depends on your natural brows and the look you want — shaping and tint tidy and define, while lamination gives a fuller, brushed-up finish. We talk it through at your appointment.
