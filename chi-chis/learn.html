Hair notes from Burlington Drive

Practical hair-care reading for Westville and the Upper Highway, from the chair at Chi Chi's Hair Studio, Dawncliffe.

Common questions

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on the cut and how fast your hair grows, but most people find every 6 to 10 weeks keeps a shape looking sharp. Shorter, precise styles usually need more frequent trims than longer looks.

How long does balayage take?

A full balayage typically takes 2 to 4 hours depending on hair length, density and how much lift is needed, plus time for toning and a finishing style.

How do I keep colour from fading?

Wash with a colour-safe, sulphate-free shampoo, keep water on the cooler side, and use UV protection if you're often outdoors — heat and hard water are the biggest causes of early fade.
