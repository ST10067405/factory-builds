our studio

Designed to transform.

entropy HAIR DESIGNS STUDIO is not your average salon. Nestled in the Lillies Quarter lifestyle centre on Old Main Road, Hillcrest, we are a full-service hair studio with an editorial edge — where technique meets creativity and every client leaves looking deliberately, undeniably themselves.

Named for the principle of creative disorder, entropy embraces individuality. Whether you are here for a precision cut, a bold colour transformation, a sleek blow wave or your first proper shave, our stylists bring the same level of care and craftsmanship to every chair.
