Good hair, between visits

Little things make a big difference to how your cut and colour hold up. Here's what the team at Synergy shares with clients, no jargon, just what actually helps.

Common questions

How often should I refresh my colour?

It depends on the technique. A full colour or root touch-up usually shows regrowth at 4–6 weeks, while balayage and lived-in colour are designed to grow out softly and can stretch to 10–14 weeks. Book a consultation and we'll map a schedule to your hair and budget.

How do I keep my balayage looking fresh for longer?

Wash less often and in cooler water, use a sulphate-free colour-safe shampoo, add a weekly toning or bonding treatment, and always use heat protection before styling. A gloss top-up between appointments keeps tones bright.

Do you offer consultations before a big colour change?

Yes. For any significant change (going lighter, a colour correction, or a fresh look) a quick consultation lets us check your hair's condition, agree on a realistic plan, and give you an accurate quote before we start.
