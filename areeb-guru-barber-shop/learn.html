Common questions

How often should I get a haircut?

Most men do well with a cut every 3–4 weeks to keep a fade sharp; looser styles can stretch to 5–6 weeks.

What's the difference between a fade and a taper?

A taper gradually shortens hair down to the skin at the neckline and sides with no hard line. A fade blends much more aggressively and higher up the head, often down to skin, for a sharper contrast.

How do I keep my beard looking sharp between trims?

Wash it a few times a week, comb it daily to train the growth direction, and use a light oil or balm to stop dryness and stray hairs.
