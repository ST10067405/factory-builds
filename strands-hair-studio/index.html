STRANDS sits inside The Colony on Old Main Road, one of Hillcrest's most considered shopping destinations. We specialise in precision cuts, lived-in colour and restorative treatments that work with your hair, not against it.

Whether you are after a clean shape-up, a full colour transformation or a treatment that actually makes a difference, we approach every service with the same care. And we never rush it.
