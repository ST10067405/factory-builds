Our Story

A Bistro Born in the Bush

Tucked away at the Morewood Activity Centre on Springside Road, The Potter Shed Bistro is Hillcrest's best-kept secret, and one of its most beloved. We opened with a simple belief: good food tastes even better outdoors, surrounded by nature.

Whether you're stopping in for a morning coffee, a hearty lunch with the family, or a lazy afternoon over craft beer and shared platters, the vibe is always the same: relaxed, welcoming and connected to the land around us.

Owner Brett and the team are passionate about keeping things honest: fresh ingredients, fair prices and a warm welcome every time. Dogs are always welcome, and so are muddy boots.
